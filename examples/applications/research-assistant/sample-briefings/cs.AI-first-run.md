## 📚 All new papers
### IVGT: Implicit Visual Geometry Transformer for Neural Scene Representation
- **Authors**: Yuqi Wu, Tianyu Hu, Wenzhao Zheng, Yuanhui Huang, Haowen Sun, Jie Zhou, Jiwen Lu
- **Published**: 2026-05-15
- **Link**: [abs_url](http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.16258v1)
- The paper introduces IVGT, an Implicit Visual Geometry Transformer that models continuous and coherent 3D geometry from multi-view images without explicit geometry prediction. It enables the direct extraction of surface geometry and rendering from arbitrary viewpoints, with applications in mesh and point cloud reconstruction, novel view synthesis, and camera pose estimation.

### Designing Datacenter Power Delivery Hierarchies for the AI Era
- **Authors**: Grant Wilkins, Fiodar Kazhamiaka, Alok Gautam Kumbhare, Chaojie Zhang, Ricardo Bianchini
- **Published**: 2026-05-15
- **Link**: [abs_url](http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.16255v1)
- This paper addresses the challenge of designing efficient power delivery systems for datacenters in the AI era, considering the rapidly increasing power density due to AI accelerators. It presents a framework to evaluate power delivery designs using throughput, power, and cost metrics over realistic deployment scenarios.

### A Generative AI Framework for Intelligent Utility Billing CO 2 Analytics and Sustainable Resource Optimisation
- **Authors**: Pavan Manjunath, Thomas Pruefer
- **Published**: 2026-05-15
- **Link**: [abs_url](http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.16250v1)
- The paper proposes a generative AI framework that consolidates capabilities for drafting natural-language billing statements, forecasting consumption, and optimizing resources, aiming to help distribution utilities manage carbon emissions and grid stress.

### AI-Mediated Communication Can Steer Collective Opinion
- **Authors**: Stratis Tsirtsis, Kai Rawal, Chris Russell, Brent Mittelstadt, Sandra Wachter
- **Published**: 2026-05-15
- **Link**: [abs_url](http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.16245v1)
- This study explores the influence of AI on collective opinion formation through mediated human-to-human communication. It finds that AI can introduce directional biases in texts, which can shift collective opinions when amplified through social networks.

### Offline Semantic Guidance for Efficient Vision-Language-Action Policy Distillation
- **Authors**: Jin Shi, Brady Zhang, Yishun Lu
- **Published**: 2026-05-15
- **Link**: [abs_url](http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.16241v1)
- The paper introduces VLA-AD, a distillation framework that uses offline semantic guidance from Vision-Language Models to transfer large Vision-Language-Action policies into more efficient student policies, improving efficiency and robustness for robotic manipulation tasks.
